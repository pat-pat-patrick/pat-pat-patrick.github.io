🧠 Where Does the Money Come From?

Financial markets are filled with people, and people have emotions. My system is a computer program devoid of feelings, designed specifically to profit when "others lose their rationality."

When sudden bad news strikes (like a geopolitical crisis), the public panics and sells at any cost, causing prices to "overshoot." The mathematical model calculates this extreme point. Like a calm and calculated recycler, it buys the asset at a steep discount when others are throwing it away in despair, and sells it back once the market regains its sanity.
