Beijing Spring Features in April

🌸 Best Flower Viewing Spots

  • Yuyuantan Park - Cherry blossom peak season (mid-late April)
  • Beihai Park - Peach blossoms and crabapple flowers in full bloom
  • Jingshan Park - Beautiful peony flowers with palace backdrop
  • Liangma River - Scenic cherry blossom night views along the waterfront

🌤️ Weather & What to Pack

Temperature: 10-20°C (50-68°F)
Temperature Variation: Large difference between day and night
Recommended Clothing: Layered clothing, bring light jacket
Essential: Comfortable walking shoes (lots of walking!)

Practical Information

⏰ Best Travel Times

  • Morning: 9:00-11:00 (avoid morning rush hour 7:30-9:00)
  • Afternoon: 14:00-16:00 (avoid noon rush hour 12:00-14:00)
  • Evening: 17:00-19:00 (avoid evening rush hour 17:30-19:00)
  • Tip: Start activities early to avoid crowds at major attractions

🎫 Useful Tips

  • Purchase a Yikatong card for easy metro and bus payments
  • Most attractions offer English signage and some staff speak English
  • Download offline maps (Amap/Baidu Maps work best in China)
  • Popular attractions may have long queues; arrive early or book online
  • Spring is peak tourist season - book accommodations and activities in advance

💳 Payment & Money

  • Cash is still widely used, especially for small vendors and local restaurants
  • Mobile payments (WeChat Pay, Alipay) are very common and convenient
  • ATMs are abundant throughout Beijing for cash withdrawal
  • Credit cards accepted at most hotels, restaurants, and shopping malls
  • Exchange rate: 1 CHF ≈ 8.3 CNY (rates vary, check current rates)
